The Story

Made in a
local kitchen.

Every jar of Schweigert's Jam is made by hand in small batches. No shortcuts. No mystery ingredients. Just the stuff that makes jam taste like it actually came from someone's kitchen — because it did.

We got into this because the farmers market deserves better than shelf-stable grocery store jam with seventeen syllable ingredient lists. Come find us Saturday morning and try a jammer. We think you'll taste the difference.

— Billy
